The Career Cycle

From application to retirement. Every decision impacts your trajectory in the simulated economy.

1. The Hunt

Craft your resume, filter through hundreds of job listings, and face AI-driven interviewers. Salary negotiation happens in real-time.

2. The Grind

Handle tickets, attend meetings, and navigate office politics. Your choices in dialogue affect your relationship with colleagues and bosses.

3. The Wealth

Don't just work for money. Invest your savings in a simulated stock market influenced by in-game news events. Buy assets and retire early.
